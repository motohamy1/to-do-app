# Phase 1: Quickstart & Testing

## Verification Steps
1. **Today View Filter**: Mark a task as "done". Verify it disappears from the "Today" list immediately. Check that the first filter chip is named "To-Do" instead of "All" (and remains "المهام" in Arabic).
2. **FAB**: Navigate to the home tab. Verify the FAB is visible at the bottom right and opens the task creation modal. Scroll the list to ensure the FAB remains fixed.
3. **Notification Sounds**: Go to Settings, change the notification sound to "Default" or "Alarm Tone". Start a 1-minute timer and wait for it to complete. Ensure the chosen sound plays. On Android, verify the notification channel was recreated with the correct sound.
